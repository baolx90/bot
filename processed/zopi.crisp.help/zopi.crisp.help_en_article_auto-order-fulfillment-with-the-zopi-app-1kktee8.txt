In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to do it.FULFILL ONE ORDER.  Log in to your account on Zopi.Go to Orders Center > To Order tab > Click on Place order to fulfill the selected order.Confirm the shipping information and continue choosing the Place order buttonWaiting for the process, Zopi will auto-fulfill the needed information and move the order to  Awaiting Payment tab. Finally, click on Pay now to confirm & payBULK FULFILL ORDERS (available for Paid PLAN users only) Kindly note that the workflow of bulk fulfill orders is prioritized fulfilled from the OLDEST order to the NEWEST one. Log in your account on AliExpress. Go to Orders List > To Order tab > click on Place All Orders button to fulfill all unfinished orders. Zopi will auto fulfill one by one order. It starts from the oldest orders.Then Zopi will auto move to fulfill the next order, until the last order.After you pay for your suppliers successfully on AliExpress, Zopi will take care of the rest to sync the tracking code to your Shopify store.In case you want to check and track your orders in Zopi, this guide has more information for you.Now you understand how to fulfill your orders through Zopi, hopefully you can have all your orders fulfilled successfully.
