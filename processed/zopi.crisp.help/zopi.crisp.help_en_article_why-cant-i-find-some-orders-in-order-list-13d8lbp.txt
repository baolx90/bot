Mark the order as paid & unfulfilled, and then check if it appeared in Order List.   Product was removed from Zopi's Listed Products.Check if the products that have orders still exist in your Listed products (Mapped).If it's still in the Listed products (Mapped), there is highly a problem with syncing orders.
