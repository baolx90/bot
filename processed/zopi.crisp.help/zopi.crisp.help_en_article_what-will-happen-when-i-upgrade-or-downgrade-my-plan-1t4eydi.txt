For example, if you begin a 30-day billing cycle on a $5.00 plan, and upgrade to a $15.00 plan on day 15 of the billing cycle, then you're charged $5.00 + ($15.00 - $5.00) * (15/30) = $10.00 USD.DOWNGRADE Downgrade plan successfully will:   REDUCE the limitation of the product which has been linked with AliExpress. Other premium functions listed above will be locked.
