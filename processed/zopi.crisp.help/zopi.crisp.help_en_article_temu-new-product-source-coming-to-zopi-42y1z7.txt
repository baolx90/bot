Let’s find out!This article contains the following sections:What is Temu?Import products from TemuFulfill orders on Temu with ZopiThings to notice when dropshipping with TemuWhat is Temu?Temu is known for its low prices and its wide selection of products, including fashion, home goods, electronics, and more. With a user-friendly website and mobile app, Temu is already an accessible and convenient platform for consumers.Create an US Temu.com account for fulfillmentImport Products from TemuYou can import products from Temu in two ways:Method 1: Import products by ExtensionThis is the simplest way. Before you begin, make sure that you have installed the Zopi Chrome extension in your Chrome browser.Go to Temu.com Click the purple Zopi icon on the product details page or click Add to import list on the product card on the product category page. Go back to Zopi > Imported products on Product Center to check your newly imported product.Method 2: Import products by Temu URLCopy the URL in the Address bar on the Temu product details page. Login to Zopi > Product Center and choose Add product by source URL.Paste the URL in the Search box and click Add to Import.Fulfill your orders on Temu with ZopiAll the orders on Temu are required to be fulfilled via the Zopi Chrome extension. Once you have an order, head to the Orders menu on the app to start the fulfillment process. Click on Place order to fulfill the selected order.Follow the process on Temu to have the order placed properly.Things to notice when dropshipping with TemuTemu allows only one Ship-to location per sign-in. If you want to change the shipping location, Temu requires you to sign in again.Currently, Zopi can only help fulfill Temu orders shipped to the United States. If your buyers are not from the US, the app will display the following notice.Orders on Temu have to be $10 - $30 minimum, depending on your account. You can contact the Temu team to learn more.Now that you’ve known all of the basics to dropship on Temu. Hopefully, all the processes go smoothly and your business can thrive.
