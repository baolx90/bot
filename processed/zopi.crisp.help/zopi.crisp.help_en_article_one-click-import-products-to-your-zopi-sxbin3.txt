No more worries, with Zopi, importing products into your Shopify store is a breeze and can be done quickly.This article covers three convenient options for importing your products:Import from Find ProductImport by source URLImport by Zopi Chrome ExtensionImport from Find ProductLogin to Zopi > Find product.Search for the items you want, using keywords or pasting the AliExpress/CJ Dropshipping product link in the search box.Hover over the desired product to import.Alternatively, click on View Detail for a comprehensive overview of the item including supplier rating, product description, etc.Click on the Add to Import List.Import by source URLCopy the URL in the Address bar on the AliExpress/Temu/CJ Dropshipping product details page.Login to Zopi > Product Center and choose Add product by source URL.Paste the URL in the Search box and click Add to Import.If successful, your products will be moved to the Imported Products section in the Product Center.Import by Zopi Chrome ExtensionBefore you begin, make sure that you have already installed the Zopi extension from Google Chrome.Go to AliExpress.com/ Temu.comClick the purple Zopi icon on AliExpress/Temu product details page or click Add to import list on the product card on the Temu product category pageAfter the product is imported successfully, you will receive a notification.Well done! You are now halfway through the process. Now you can go to Imported products on the Product Center to check your newly imported products.Hope this article helps you determine how you can use Zopi as one of the best companion for your dropshipping business.
