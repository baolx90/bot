That means you have to change the address before you fulfill order.There are two methods that you can utilize to change the addresses on the orders:1. On Shopify admin page. Kindly follow the steps below to change the new shipping address:   Go to the Shopify dashboard > Orders > Find the order > Click on the order to open a manage order window.  Click on the "..." and choose "Edit shipping address".  Change the shipping address and click on the "Save" button. 2. On the Zopi appYou can edit the Customer's address information straight on the Zopi app.First, please go to the Orders and take a look at the outstanding orders that need fulfilling.Then, click on the Customer's name to start changing / correcting the information.You might find these articles interesting:Getting the Order Tracking Code with AliExpress (AE) automaticallyStatus of your ORDERSAuto Order Fulfillment with the Zopi appIf you have any further concerns or issues, please do not hesitate to contact us via the Live chat window on the right screen or email us via support@zopi.ioUpdated on: 28/02/2024Was this article helpful?YesNoShare your feedbackSend My FeedbackCancelThank you!Not finding what you are looking for?Chat with us or send us an email.Chat with us© 2024 Zopi Help DeskWe run on Crisp Knowledge.
