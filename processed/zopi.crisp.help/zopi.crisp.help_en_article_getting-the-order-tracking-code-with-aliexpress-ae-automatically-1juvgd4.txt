From now on, ALL the order tracking codes will be automatically synced to Zopi, and sent to your customers by the time it is generated on AliExpress.   When you log in to Zopi app, one of the first important step is to connect with your AliExpress account: By clicking AliExpress button, you have accepted Zopi to use the access token of your AliExpress account to keep it sync the data in real-time without login again. All is done from here, the status of this button will change to green, which means your account is linked. You can change to another AliExpress account by disconnect and reconnect a new one:From now, all the tracking codes of your orders will be synced in real time with your AliExpress account.
