This product will be found in the Imported products section of the Product Center. Here you can keep all original information, or you can customize it to look more professional. Once done, the next step is to push the products from Zopi to your store.In this guide, you'll learn: How to customize product detailsHow to push products to Shopify storeHow to customize product detailsClick on the product name or select the pen symbol in the actions area. This will open the product editor page, where you can access various tabs to edit the product details. Refer to this guide for detailed instructions on customizing product details. How to push products to Shopify storeNow you can push well-customized products to your store by following these steps: From the Zopi dashboard > Product Center > Imported productsHover on the specific product and click the  Push to Store button to push an individual product or check the box on the Product Card and click on Push all products to bulk push products.IMPORTANT NOTE:* If you are editing product details in the Product Editor window, you can also directly click the Push to store button to push your products.* To unlock the Push all products feature, you’ll need to upgrade to the Starter plan or higher.In the pop-up, consider checking the box next to each function if needed:Push as Unpublish ProductContinue selling when out of stockClick the Push to store button to confirm.IMPORTANT NOTE: The product will become visible to all your customers if you directly publish it to your online store. If you only push as Unpublished Products, it will appear in the backend of your store where you can edit or manage it before making it visible to all your customers. Learn more here.Once you've done all that, you can go to Listed products (Mapped) to see the products you just pushed.
