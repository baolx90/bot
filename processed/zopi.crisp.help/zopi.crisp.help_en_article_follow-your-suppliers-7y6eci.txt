Following suppliers makes it easy to update the latest information from chosen suppliers, so you can select the most appropriate products to sell. This guide will walk you through the steps required to use this feature effectively:Accessing the Follow supplier featureDiscovering the Followed supplier(s) listLet's get started!Accessing the Follow supplier featureLog into the Zopi app.From the Find Product page, click on a specific product and it will direct you to the Product Detail.Here, scroll down and you can see the Follow Supplier button.Discovering the Followed supplier(s) listOnce this is done, suppliers that you've followed will be added to the Followed supplier(s) list in the upper right corner of the Find Product page.If you find any product which has potential, you can hit the Add to Import button to import directly from this followed supplier(s) list. We will sync the data of your supplier’s products every 24 hours, so you'll always have the latest information.Our goal is to enhance your experience and boost your chances of finding high-potential suppliers to dropship.
