Well, there is! Order settings is a powerful tool that can help you easily streamline the fulfillment process and boost your profit margin.  All you need to do is go to Zopi Dashboard -> Orders  -> Order settings.  From here, you can select AliExpress/Temu source to set up order-based on the highlighted section below: Default ShipmentWhen the order is placed in AliExpress/Temu, that shipping method will be automatically chosen. There are 2 options for each section: Cheapest method: Zopi will pick up the cheapest shipping price on the list.Fastest method: some of these methods are faster than others, so we will compare different shipping options and offer you the fastest one. Specific method: this is the shipping method you want to apply to all of your orders.You can choose the cheapest method or Specific method that you trust (Etc: AliExpress Standard Shipping, UPS, ePacket,...). The priority for choosing the Specific shipping method will be the Main shipping method first, and then the Backup shipping method.If specific shipping methods are unavailable based on your setup, Zopi will optimize for the Cheapest Method.Default Fulfill Information1. Auto fill customer information.Sometimes the buyer did not provide full order information, which may lead to order failure. However, you can avoid this by setting default customer information, which will be automatically applied to orders when placing them with your suppliers.For orders shipped to Chile or Brazil, we will automatically populate RUT numbers or CPF numbers when they are missing from the company field.Phone number is always required by AliExpress. However, if you don’t have your customer’s phone number, you can easily set your own default number by choosing the backup option Use customer info, then input the numbers into the box.In case any of the fields (City, Province, Address, Apartment, Postal Code) is missing in the Country field, we will apply all the information you set.Note: We are currently only able to help with fulfilling Temu orders shipped to the United States. As a result, the Order settings section for Temu source will default to US for the phone numbers and Country fields.2. Auto Add Checkout Note To AliExpress Suppliers.You can also set up the default message to all the suppliers when you place the order on AliExpress to notify them that you are doing dropshipping.
