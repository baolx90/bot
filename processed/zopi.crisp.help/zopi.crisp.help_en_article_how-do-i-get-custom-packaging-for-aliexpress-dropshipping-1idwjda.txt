This will make your store more unique and help you stand out from the competition.One of the simplest methods is to message directly your supplier in AliExpress and agree on the packaging. Once you have found the supplier that you are happy with, you can collaborate with them.Remember to keep these things in mind:Having a good contact with your AliExpress supplier.
