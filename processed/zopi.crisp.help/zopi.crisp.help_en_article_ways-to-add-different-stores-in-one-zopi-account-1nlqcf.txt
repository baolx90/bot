In some scenarios, you may need to manage all your products and orders in one place. Zopi allows you to manage all your online stores from a single account, streamlining your dropshipping experience and increasing efficiency.In this article, we will provide instructions for setting up a new store. Before we get started, take note that there are a few common requirements:Add a new free store to a paid store.Add a new free store to another free store.Now, let’s cut to the chase to see how to do so.In the Shopify embedded app:In the Zopi dashboard, go to the Settings & Billing page → AccountClick Link accountEnter your information to log in, or sign in with your Google/Facebook account, then click on Confirm button.In case you’re unable to add the store for any reason, don’t hesitate to contact our Support team for assistance.In the web appIn the Zopi dashboard, go to the Settings & Billing page → AccountClick Add storeEnter the Shopify store URL of the shop you want to connectIMPORTANT NOTE:* The store you want to add has either removed the app or has not been linked to any account before.* Zopi app is currently optimized for Shopify, when you want to sign in to Zopi web app, please connect with our Support team to update your email internally first.The good news is, you can connect an unlimited number of stores under a single Zopi account.
