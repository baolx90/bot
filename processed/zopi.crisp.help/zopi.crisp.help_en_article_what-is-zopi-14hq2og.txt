You may be new to the dropshipping game. You may be overwhelmed to all of this. Say no more! Let us walk you through the things you need to know about Zopi - your dropshipping companion. Let’s get started!We basically assist you to import the products from AliExpress/Temu/CJ Dropshipping straight in the app. Then, you will have the freedom to edit the product price/variants/image/shipping info (shipping fee, country, etc.) and split variants into separate products in 1 click. After that, you’ll be able to push these products into your Shopify store to sell as dropshipping.AliExpress Product ListFirst, you will stumble upon the list of products that Zopi retrieves directly from AliExpress/[CJ Dropshipping](https://zopi.crisp.help/en/article/cj-dropshipping-new-resource-for-your-store-1i7dx1u/). Here is where you will get the ideas of what to sell on your store. Identify the trending products or the best-selling ones by looking at metrics like Latest Orders or Reviews. In case you already have what you want in mind, this is where to analyze the products and determine if those ideas are worth following.Product DetailClicking on any product will lead you to the Product Detail page. It is best to check all the details here including Supplier credibility, Product description, Reviews from buyers, … before deciding to add the product to the Imported products.Tip: We highly recommend checking the Pricing Rules first, located in the Imported products menu before adding any product to the Imported products. Learn more hereImported ProductsOnce you have clicked Add to Import List, the products will go here. This is a waiting list for your products before getting publicly published on your Shopify store. Here you can review them carefully before publishing them. Order FulfillmentYou’ve got orders on Shopify and don’t know what to do? The next step is to order those items on AliExpress/Temu/CJ Dropshipping. Let Zopi handle it for you, nice and easy.All you have to do is click on Place Order to fulfill the selected order. Zopi will bring the information to AliExpress/Temu/CJ Dropshipping and automatically place the order for you. After the process is done, you’d need to follow the instructions to complete the Payment steps.Above is the information you need to start using Zopi.
